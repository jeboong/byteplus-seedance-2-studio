# Introduction

* ModelArk's content pre-filter includes a deep learning based feature offered in conjunction with ModelArk's generative AI services that enables customers to block the generation of images and voices that resemble public figures across a number of categories, such as politics, sports, business, entertainment, and media. This feature helps prevent fraud and deepfakes.
* This feature analyzes AI-generated outputs to determine whether a detected face or voice is similar to that of a public figure included in the feature's reference set. It is powered by our face and voice detection and similarity technology. If a match is identified, the content may be blocked, filtered, or otherwise prevented from being delivered to the end user.
* Please note that this feature is not an authority on, and in no way purports to be, a comprehensive block on prominent public figures. The feature has been designed to protect against impersonation, fraud, and deceptive deepfake content, and may change depending on product needs and customer feedback. 
* If a public figure wishes to be removed from or added to this feature, they can send a request to us by contacting [privacy@byteplus.com](mailto:privacy@byteplus.com) and we will process the request following appropriate confirmation.
